Careers at bumble

Join our team and help revolutionize entertainment

Work With Us

At bumble, we're building the future of synchronized entertainment. We're looking for talented, passionate individuals who want to make an impact in the streaming and social entertainment space. If you're excited about innovation, community, and creating amazing experiences, we'd love to hear from you.

Our Culture

We believe in fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and innovative work environment where every team member can thrive. Our values guide everything we do:

Innovation

We push boundaries and embrace new ideas to create cutting-edge solutions.

Collaboration

We work together across teams to achieve common goals and support each other's growth.

Community

We're committed to building safe, inclusive communities where everyone belongs.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from product to customer service.

Open Positions

We're currently hiring for several positions. Check back regularly for new opportunities or contact us at careers@bumbleapp.com to learn about upcoming roles.

Senior Software Engineer

Full-time • Remote

We're looking for experienced software engineers to help build and scale our platform. Experience with real-time synchronization, streaming technologies, and distributed systems is a plus.

Product Manager

Full-time • Remote

Join our product team to shape the future of bumble. We're seeking a strategic product manager with experience in social platforms and entertainment technology.

Community Manager

Full-time • Remote

Help build and nurture our vibrant community. We're looking for passionate community managers who can engage with users, moderate discussions, and foster positive interactions.

Benefits & Perks

We take care of our team members with competitive benefits including:

  • Competitive salary and equity packages
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage
  • Flexible work arrangements and remote options
  • Professional development and learning opportunities
  • Generous time off and wellness programs
  • Collaborative and inclusive work environment

Get In Touch

Interested in joining the bumble team? Send your resume and a brief cover letter to careers@bumbleapp.com. We review all applications and will be in touch if your background aligns with our needs.
