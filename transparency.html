Transparency Report

At bumble, we believe in being transparent with our community about how we operate, the decisions we make, and the actions we take to keep our platform safe and secure. This transparency report provides insights into our platform's operations, safety measures, and community management efforts.

1. Platform Operations

bumble operates as a synchronized watch party platform designed to bring people together through shared entertainment experiences. Our platform enables users to watch content from multiple streaming services simultaneously with friends and communities around the world.

We maintain strict operational standards to ensure the platform remains secure, reliable, and accessible to all users. Our infrastructure is designed to handle millions of concurrent users while maintaining data integrity and security.

2. Safety and Moderation

Safety is our top priority. We employ a combination of automated systems and human moderators to monitor platform activity and enforce our community guidelines. Our moderation team works 24/7 to identify and address violations.

  • Automated content filtering systems scan for prohibited content
  • User reporting mechanisms allow community members to flag violations
  • Human moderators review flagged content and take appropriate action
  • Regular audits ensure consistent enforcement of policies
  • Collaboration with law enforcement on serious violations

3. Community Guidelines Enforcement

We enforce our community guidelines consistently and fairly. Violations can result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans depending on severity and frequency.

In 2025, our moderation team reviewed over 50,000 reports and took action on approximately 85% of them. We continue to refine our processes to ensure fair and consistent enforcement.

4. Data Protection and Privacy

We are committed to protecting user data and privacy. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with international data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

Our privacy practices are detailed in our Privacy Policy. We do not sell user data to third parties and only share information when necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements.

5. User Feedback and Improvements

We actively listen to user feedback and use it to improve our platform. In 2025, we implemented over 200 feature requests and improvements based on community feedback.

Users can submit feedback through our support portal, and we review all submissions. Major changes are communicated to the community through our blog and social media channels.

6. Incident Response

When security incidents or service disruptions occur, we respond quickly and transparently. We maintain an incident response team that can address issues within minutes.

We communicate with affected users about incidents and provide updates on resolution efforts. Post-incident reports are published to help the community understand what happened and how we prevented similar issues.

7. Third-Party Partnerships

bumble partners with various third-party services to enhance our platform. All partners are vetted for security and privacy compliance. We maintain strict data sharing agreements with all partners.

We do not share user data with partners without explicit user consent, except where necessary to provide our services.

8. Accessibility and Inclusivity

We are committed to making bumble accessible to all users. Our platform supports multiple languages and accessibility features for users with disabilities.

We continuously work to improve accessibility and welcome feedback from users about how we can better serve diverse communities.

9. Contact and Questions

For questions about this transparency report or our operations, please contact us:

Email: support@bumbleapp.com
