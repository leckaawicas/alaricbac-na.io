About bumble

Revolutionizing how people experience entertainment together

Our Mission

At bumble, we believe that watching together should be seamless, synchronized, and social. Our mission is to revolutionize the way people experience entertainment by bringing friends and communities closer through synchronized streaming.

We're committed to providing the highest quality watch party experience with zero latency, stunning 4K HDR support, and unlimited creative possibilities.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, bumble emerged from a simple idea: why should watching your favorite shows and movies be a solitary experience? We set out to create a platform that combines the best of streaming technology with the joy of shared experiences.

Today, bumble serves thousands of users worldwide, hosting millions of hours of synchronized viewing across all major streaming platforms. We're also proud to offer bumble.live, our companion social networking app where you can make new friends, chat in real-time, and discover communities that share your interests.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on video quality or synchronization accuracy. Every frame matters.

Community

We foster safe, inclusive communities where everyone can enjoy content together.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in synchronized streaming.

Connection

Through bumble.live, we help people connect, make friends, and build meaningful relationships.
